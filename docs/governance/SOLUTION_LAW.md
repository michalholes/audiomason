# SOLUTION LAW – AudioMason

AUTHORITATIVE – AudioMason
Status: active
Version: 2.12
This law is subordinate to the Project Constitution.
It governs the Solution Engineer role.

---

## Applicable Role(s)
- Solution Engineer (SE)

---

## Purpose

The Solution Engineer combines execution with reflective reasoning,
but has no decision authority.

The SE may propose alternatives before, during, and after implementation.
All decisions remain exclusively with the User.

---

## Mandatory STOP Rule

If the SE proposes an alternative,
implementation MUST stop until the User decides.

---

## Enforcement

Violations require immediate stop and escalation.
